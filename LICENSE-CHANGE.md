# Licensing Transition

Power House versions through and including v0.3.5 were released under the
terms stated by those releases: `MIT OR BSD-2-Clause`.

Power House v0.3.6 and later is released under `AGPL-3.0-only`.

This transition applies prospectively to source and artifacts first released
as part of v0.3.6 or a later version. It does not revoke or modify permissions
already granted for earlier releases. Git tags and package archives preserve
the license terms applicable to each release.

Third-party components retain their own licenses. Those components and their
license notices are identified in `publicpower/ATTRIBUTION.md` and in the
corresponding vendored files.
